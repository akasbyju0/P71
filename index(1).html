Review on the Movies

Fast and Furious 6

Furious 6 is basically a "round up the gang" western where the outlaws (Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Tyrese Gibson, Ludacris, Sung Kang, and Gal Gadot) are the only ones who can help their former lawman adversary (Dwayne Johnson back as DSS Agent Luke Hobbs) track down an even worse criminal: former British special forces soldier Owen Shaw (Luke Evans), who is pulling off a series of stunning auto-centric crimes. As the saying goes, it takes a thief to catch a thief so Hobbs approaches his Fast 5 foes for help. Hobbs has one very big incentive for Dom Toretto and his crew to agree to the job (besides full pardons so they can return to the US): Dom's flame Letty (Michelle Rodriguez), presumed dead in the fourth film Fast & Furious, is actually alive and serving as a member of Shaw's gang. Dom and the team set out to stop Shaw and bring Letty back home.
